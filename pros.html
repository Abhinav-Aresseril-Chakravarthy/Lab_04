Cpt. John Price Captain John Price was a British SAS Sniper and one of the main Protagonists of the Call of Duty Modern Warfare Series. He was also the head of Task Force 141, an elite unit under the command of General Shepherd Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2019 Source: Image from wikipedia
Viktor Reznov Viktor Reznov was a Russian Soldier who fought during World War II and Cold War era. He was an important character to both Dimitri Petrenko in World at War and Alex Mason in Black Ops 1.He was killed off in Vokruta during the events of Black Ops 1. Simon Riley - Ghost Simon Riley aka Ghost is a character in Modern Warfare II. He was recruited into Task Force 141 by Captain Price. He is helps Soap Mactavish in the Favela in Brazil and in trying to get information about Makarov in his Mansion. He was betrayed by Shepherd along with Roach during escape.
